Introduction

This is my "About Me" page. Hope you like it!

My wife and three children help me run our small business making art supplies. I'm also pursuing a computer science degree with what little spare time I have.

I have an Associate's degree in Computer Engineering Technology, and have worked as a computer programmer in the past. As my kids came along, I felt that being there for them was more important than computer programming, and I have been making art supplies for the last 12 years. Now that they're older, I'm ready to jump back into development!

The most important thing for me is keeping my family taken care of, preferably with a job that is satisfying and rewarding.
